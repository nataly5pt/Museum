Our Staff

Meet the team behind the exhibits, fieldwork, and preservation efforts.

Dr. Amina Selim

Chief Curator & Field Director

Oversees acquisitions and leads seasonal surveys along the inland Sahel corridor. Amina champions community-first conservation and open access to findings.

Mei Lin

Registrar

Riley Thompson
